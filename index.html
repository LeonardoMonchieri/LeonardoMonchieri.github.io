PIOF

    Made with:

PIOF is an IoT device that provide a website and a local station to manage the watering of a garden. It's possible to set a daily or weekly periodic watering and also the board monitor the weather and adapt the watering schedule with it. The boards used for this system are two ESP8266: one as weather station and the other as local controller/web server.
